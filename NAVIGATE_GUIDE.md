# 🏟️ StadiumIQ AI - Step-by-Step Navigation & User Guide

Welcome to the FIFA World Cup 2026 Smart Stadium & Tournament Operations Platform. This document provides step-by-step guidance on how to start the platform and navigate through its various features.

---

## 🛠️ Step 1: Launch the Services

To open and run the website locally on your computer, use the provided custom launcher script:

1. Locate the workspace folder: `c:\Users\RENU\StadiumIQ AI`.
2. Double-click the **`run.bat`** file.
3. This script will automatically launch two terminal windows:
   - **Terminal 1**: Runs the Python FastAPI Backend on port `8000`.
   - **Terminal 2**: Runs the Next.js Frontend Dev server on port `3000`.
4. Leave both terminals open while using the website.

---

## 🌐 Step 2: Open the Website in Your Browser

Once the command prompts show that the services are active:
1. Open your web browser (Chrome, Edge, Firefox, or Safari).
2. Enter the following address in the URL bar:
   👉 **[http://localhost:3000](http://localhost:3000)**
3. Press **Enter**. You will see the glowing green and blue StadiumIQ AI homepage dashboard load instantly.

---

## 🎮 Step 3: Navigating the Dashboards & Features

### 👤 1. Changing User Roles
At the top right of the screen next to the settings, locate the **User Role Selector** dropdown. You can switch between 6 roles:
* **Fan**: Plan stadium routes, check matches, lookup food, use Lost & Found, and consult the smart map.
* **Volunteer**: Report lost children, access real-time translation help, submit incident logs, and check shift timers.
* **Security Staff**: Generate official AI incident summaries and monitor crowd flow trend lines.
* **Organizer**: Read the daily morning operational briefing report synthesized by Gemini.
* **Transport Manager**: View metro/bus shuttle waiting times and transit share charts.
* **Stadium Administrator**: Manage carbon metrics, recycling, and green energy optimizations.

---

### 💬 2. Multilingual AI Assistant (Right Sidebar Tab)
1. Select the **AI Assistant** tab on the right sidebar.
2. Select your preferred language from the dropdown (supports English, Español, Français, العربية, हिन्दी, Português).
3. Type any stadium query (e.g. *"Where is Entry Gate 4?"* or *"Suggest vegan food"*) and click **Send** (or press Enter).
4. Click the **Microphone** icon to simulate speech-to-text queries.
5. If **Voice Assistant (Volume icon)** is toggled on at the top header, the AI will speak its responses aloud.

---

### 🚨 3. Activating Emergency Evacuation Mode
1. Click the red **🚨 EVAC** button in the header.
2. The entire webpage header will blink red to alert you.
3. The dashboard will automatically update to show evacuation exits, safe zones, and green path routes.
4. Click **Reset Arena Status** in the red emergency header to return to normal operation mode.

---

### ⚙️ 4. Theme & Accessibility Customization
* **Light / Dark Mode**: Click the **Sun / Moon** icon in the header to change the lighting theme.
* **High Contrast (HC)**: Click the **HC** button to activate high-contrast color styling (black background with bright yellow text) for visually impaired users.
